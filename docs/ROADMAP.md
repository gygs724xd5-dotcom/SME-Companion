# Roadmap

## Sprint 1: AI Response Intelligence

- Planner-first response routing.
- Response guard and repetition guard.
- Context-aware response selection.
- Missing information collector.
- Business memory reuse.
- Conversation continuation.

## Sprint 2: Chat Experience

- Minimal assistant indicator.
- Streaming assistant responses.
- Thinking/pulse styling.
- Suggested replies and quick actions.
- Natural conversation spacing.

## Sprint 3: Mobile First UX

- Keep homepage focused on Morning Brief, Dashboard Summary, Today's Tasks, Chat, and Recent Business Alerts.
- Move infrequent store/admin sections into sidebar.
- Reduce scroll depth.

## Sprint 4: Security

- Login/logout/session.
- Store-scoped persistence.
- No anonymous restore of previous manual store.
- Database-backed auth remains required before public production.

## Sprint 5: OCR + Inventory Foundation

- Receipt and invoice document models.
- External OCR adapter boundary.
- Product, inventory, revenue, expense, and stock movement foundations.
- No fake OCR.

## Sprint 6: Translation

- Translation request boundary for Thai, English, Arabic, and Chinese.
- Surfaces: PDF, invoice, chat, product description.

## Sprint 7: Business Memory

- Structured memory for store, products, prices, customers, suppliers, promotions, inventory, and goals.

## Sprint 8: Business Operating System

- Operate core business workflows through chat.
