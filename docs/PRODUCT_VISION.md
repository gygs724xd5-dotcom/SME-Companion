# Product Vision

SME Companion helps real SME owners operate their business through conversation.

The owner should be able to ask:

- Create today's post.
- Check inventory.
- Summarize today's sales.
- Generate quotation.
- Translate invoice.
- Create monthly report.
- What products are almost out of stock?

The long-term product is not a chat toy. It is an AI employee connected to store memory, products, prices, suppliers, customers, inventory, revenue, expenses, and business goals.
