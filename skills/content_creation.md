# Content Creation Skill

## Purpose
Plan content ideas, captions, and campaign angles from store context and user intent.

## Required Inputs
- Product or business type
- Target customer when available
- Tone when available
- Recent topics when available

## Output
- Content angle
- Suggested post structure
- Caption direction
- Next content action

## Workflow
1. Identify product, business type, and customer.
2. Check recent topics to avoid repetition.
3. Route to content workflow when deterministic fields are available.
4. Use LLM only for response wording when enabled.

## Limitations
- Does not publish posts.
- Does not generate images or videos.
- Does not schedule content externally.
