# Sales Planning Skill

## Purpose
Create a short-term sales plan from store profile, product, capacity, quantity, sales channel, and selling window.

## Required Inputs
- Product or offer to sell
- Daily capacity or available quantity
- Selling window or sales channel
- Store profile when available

## Output
- Sales goal framing
- Daily action plan
- Suggested sales angle
- Practical next step

## Workflow
1. Confirm the product or offer.
2. Confirm available quantity or daily capacity.
3. Confirm selling window or sales channel.
4. Route to the existing sales plan workflow.
5. Use LLM only if Developer Mode allows workflow enhancement.

## Limitations
- Does not forecast demand.
- Does not sync actual sales data.
- Does not change inventory or prices.
