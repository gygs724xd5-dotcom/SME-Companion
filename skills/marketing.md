# Marketing Skill

## Purpose
Plan practical marketing actions and campaign angles from current store context.

## Required Inputs
- Product or campaign objective
- Target customer
- Store tone
- Recent topics or promotions when available

## Output
- Marketing angle
- Suggested campaign direction
- Practical next action

## Workflow
1. Identify campaign intent.
2. Reuse store profile and recent topics.
3. Prefer existing content and sales planning capabilities.
4. Use LLM only when the orchestrator decides wording or explanation adds value.

## Limitations
- Does not buy ads.
- Does not publish campaigns.
- Does not automate customer outreach.
