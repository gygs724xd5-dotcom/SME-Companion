# Roadmap

SME Companion is progressing from a focused business companion into a broader AI operating layer for small and medium-sized businesses.

## Phase Overview

| Phase | Name | Status |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Phase 1 | Business Companion | Completed |
| Phase 2 | Conversation Intelligence | Completed |
| Phase 3 | Product Brain | In Progress |
| Phase 4 | Developer Console | Planned |
| Phase 5 | AI Product Manager | Future |
| Phase 6 | Business Operating System | Long-term |

## Phase 1: Business Companion

| Field | Detail |
| --- | --- |
| Goal | Help SME owners describe their business and receive practical content, promotion, sales, and business guidance. |
| Features | Store profiles, generated content plans, sales briefs, content calendar, promotion ideas, sales strategy, business diagnosis, business insights, business goals, and demo store profiles. |
| Status | Completed |
| Expected outcome | A useful first companion experience that can guide an SME owner through daily marketing and business actions. |

## Phase 2: Conversation Intelligence

| Field | Detail |
| --- | --- |
| Goal | Turn the app from a form-driven assistant into a conversational business companion. |
| Features | Conversation intent detection, chat companion responses, business-context-aware answers, response cleaning, playbook routing, follow-up handling, correction handling, reset handling, and session conversation memory. |
| Status | Completed |
| Expected outcome | A chat experience that understands user intent and can answer with relevant business context instead of generic assistant output. |

## Phase 3: Product Brain

| Field | Detail |
| --- | --- |
| Goal | Let SME Companion learn from user feedback and convert product signals into structured development insight. |
| Features | Product feedback intent detection, feedback classification, local feedback log, product backlog updates, duplicate merging, priority assignment, severity detection, trend summaries, and developer feedback summary. |
| Status | In Progress |
| Expected outcome | A feedback loop where product issues and feature requests are captured from real user conversations and become visible to developers. |

## Phase 4: Developer Console

| Field | Detail |
| --- | --- |
| Goal | Give builders a clearer operating surface for reviewing feedback, backlog items, product trends, and release readiness. |
| Features | Developer feedback views, backlog review workflow, issue status tracking, exportable summaries, release checklist visibility, and deployment health indicators. |
| Status | Planned |
| Expected outcome | Faster product iteration with less manual inspection of local JSON files and chat history. |

## Phase 5: AI Product Manager

| Field | Detail |
| --- | --- |
| Goal | Use accumulated product learning to recommend what to build, improve, or validate next. |
| Features | Product opportunity detection, sprint recommendation, issue clustering, impact estimation, roadmap suggestions, and release-note drafting. |
| Status | Future |
| Expected outcome | SME Companion can help its own builders prioritize product work from evidence rather than scattered feedback. |

## Phase 6: Business Operating System

| Field | Detail |
| --- | --- |
| Goal | Expand the companion into an operating layer for SME decisions, memory, actions, and recurring business workflows. |
| Features | Persistent business memory, richer goals, operating state, multi-source business signals, external integrations, API access, and role-specific workflows. |
| Status | Long-term |
| Expected outcome | SME owners can use SME Companion as a practical business operating system, not just a chat assistant or content generator. |
